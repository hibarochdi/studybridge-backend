🏅 Funding Your Future

Scholarship Programs

Fully-funded opportunities exist. We find them for you and support your application every step of the way.

🇨🇳
CSC Scholarship
Chinese Government Scholarship
Fully FundedBachelor/Master/PhDDeadline: March
  • Full tuition + accommodation covered
  • Monthly living stipend
  • Medical insurance included
  • 500+ partner universities in China
  • Open to all international students
How to Apply
  1. Choose a Chinese university & program
  2. Prepare dossier (transcripts, passport, medical)
  3. Apply via CSC portal before March deadline
  4. Wait for acceptance letter (2–3 months)
🇰🇷
GKS Scholarship
Global Korea Scholarship
Fully FundedUndergrad/GraduateDeadline: Feb/Mar
  • Tuition fees fully waived
  • Monthly allowance up to ₩900,000
  • Round-trip airfare covered
  • Korean language training (1 year)
  • Health insurance provided
How to Apply
  1. Check eligibility (GPA 2.64/4.0 minimum)
  2. Apply via your country's embassy OR Korean university
  3. Submit academic records, motivation letter, references
  4. Interview (if shortlisted)
🇩🇪
DAAD Scholarship
German Academic Exchange Service
Partially FundedMaster/PhDVarious Deadlines
  • Monthly stipend €850 – €1,200
  • Health, accident & liability insurance
  • Travel subsidy included
  • German language course support
How to Apply
  1. Find a DAAD scholarship matching your field
  2. Prepare: CV, research proposal, references, transcripts
  3. Apply via DAAD portal (deadlines vary Aug–Oct)
  4. Language test results (German B2 or IELTS 6.0)
🇹🇷
Türkiye Burslari
Turkish Government Scholarship
Fully FundedAll LevelsDeadline: February
  • Full tuition fee coverage
  • Monthly stipend + free accommodation
  • Turkish language course (1 year free)
  • Round-trip flight included
  • Open to 180+ countries
How to Apply
  1. Register at turkiyeburslari.gov.tr (January–February)
  2. Select up to 12 universities & programs
  3. Upload documents (diploma, ID, transcripts)
  4. Online interview if shortlisted
🇫🇷
Eiffel Excellence
French Government Scholarship
Partially FundedMaster/PhDDeadline: January
  • Monthly stipend €1,181 (Master) / €1,400 (PhD)
  • Housing allowance included
  • Round-trip flights per year
  • Health insurance coverage
How to Apply
  1. Be nominated by a French institution (not self-apply)
  2. Get accepted to a French university first
  3. Have your university submit the Eiffel dossier
  4. Results announced March–April
🇲🇦
Maroc Study Grant
Moroccan Government for International Students
Partial AidAll LevelsOpen
  • Tuition reduction for eligible nationalities
  • Accommodation in university residences
  • Access to CNOPS health insurance
  • Student meal subsidies
How to Apply
  1. Apply for admission to a Moroccan university
  2. Request scholarship via the Moroccan embassy
  3. Submit academic records and motivation letter
  4. Confirmation via Ministry of Education
